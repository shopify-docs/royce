# Royce Theme

The Royce theme gives fashion-focused Shopify stores a chic and contemporary look. Our guide has been thoughtfully designed to provide you with the best instructions for effortlessly establishing your business. Using our years of Shopify experience, we developed Royce to fully utilize the platform while taking into account the particular requirements of the fashion retail industry.

The purpose of these instructions is to help you along the way. Fashion e-commerce frequently calls for careful modification and setup clarity, even though we intend our themes to be simple and easy to use. With over 10 years of expertise in the fashion and e-commerce industries, our documentation is purposefully opinionated in some areas to ensure you get insights that go beyond the fundamentals.

**Important Features:**

* **Portfolio Layout**: Showcase your work using masonry or grid layouts that accentuate your artistic abilities.
* **Customizable Gallery**: Provides thorough views of each item, allows you to submit photos with ease, and groups them into collections.
* **Responsive Design:** optimized for desktop and mobile viewing, guaranteeing that your artwork appears fantastic across all platforms.
* **Product Showcase:** Sell prints, artwork, and other creative things straight from your business by combining your portfolio with a product showcase.
* **Clean And Minimalist Style:** Make sure your design is clean and uncomplicated so it doesn't distract from your work.
* **Header Options:** Select the header layout that best fits your branding requirements.
* **Color Scheme Customization:** The theme's colors can be altered to reflect your business or creative aesthetic.
* **Integrated Social Media Links:** In order to interact with your audience, include social media icons that connect to your Instagram, Pinterest, and other sites
