# Colors

You can alter the look of your store by establishing color schemes that specify the visual style of different parts of your website. This makes it simple to change themes across various sections of your website and preserve consistent branding.

* Go to **Online Store**> Click **Theme Settings** icon
* Locate **Color** on the left-side
* Make necessary changes.
* Click **Save.**
