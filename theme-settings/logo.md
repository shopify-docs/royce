# Logo

Use the favicon and logo options to customize the branding of your website. All pages and browser tabs will consistently display the brand thanks to these components.

* Go to **Online Store**> Click **Theme Settings** icon.
* Locate **Logo**> **Upload Images.**
* Click **Save.**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_52 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

| Logo    | Upload logo image.                                                                                                                                                     |
| ------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Width   | Adjust the logo width to fit your header layout.                                                                                                                       |
| Favicon | Upload your favicon – a small icon displayed in browser tabs and bookmarks. Size Info Will be scaled down to 32 × 32px, so use a clear, square image for best results. |
