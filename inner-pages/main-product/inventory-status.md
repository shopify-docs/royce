# Inventory Status

The **Inventory Status** section shows real-time stock availability for a product. It helps inform customers whether an item is in stock, low in quantity, or sold out, creating transparency and encouraging timely purchases.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot_138.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Low inventory threshold: Adjust the range of the inventory status. Also, check the info.

Show inventory count: Enable to show the inventory count.
